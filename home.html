Looking for a beginner workout at home equipment?The 28-day workout challenge is a great way to stop making excess:
  • No equipment required
  • Simple excercises even total beginners can do
  • Short,under 15-minute workouts
  • Only 3 excercises per workout -easy to remember
  • Only 3 workouts per week
  • Time-based workouts-adjust intensity to your level
  • 28-day [4 weeks] duration is ideal to create a healthy habit

WHAT ARE THE RULES FOR THE 28-DAY WORKOUT CHALLENGE FOR BEGINNERS?

This is simple home workout challenge doesn't have many rules:

  • Do all 3 workouts each week.Choose any day/time that suits you.
  • Do as many reps as you can with good form.Write down the number of reps and see if you can do more next week.
  • Rest 30 secs to 1 minute between rounds.Add some rest between the excercise if needed.
